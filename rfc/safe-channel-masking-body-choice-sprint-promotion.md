## 5. What the Sprint Proved

Core arc:

```text
A safe channel is not the same as a perfect channel.
Masking can be compressed regulation.
The body may choose when earlier choice disappeared.
```

The sprint establishes:

- safe channels are harm-reduction tools, not justice itself;
- small or hidden regulation is still regulation;
- masking can be strategy, armor, and cost at the same time;
- unmasking must not become another demand in unsafe rooms;
- shutdown, meltdown, collapse, or overload should be read across the whole timeline, not only at the moment other people noticed;
- accountability and repair can be named without reader-blaming.
