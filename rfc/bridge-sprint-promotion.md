## 5. Open Citation / Evidence Flags

These flags do not block promotion.

They must be resolved before the book goes to any external reader, editor, clinical reviewer, or publication path.

### Mortality / Lifespan Claim

Flag:

```text
Research increasingly shows that Autistic people face shorter average lifespans.
```

Needs:

- source citation;
- scope definition;
- population / geography clarity;
- methodological humility;
- distinction between average lifespan, mortality risk, suicide risk, co-occurring conditions, and social determinants.

### Community Longevity Analogy

Flag:

```text
researchers studying long-lived communities / patterns built into daily life
```

Needs:

- source support if retained;
- methodological humility note if specific research traditions or brands are invoked later;
- careful handling so analogy does not imply simple lifestyle fixes.

### Behavior-as-Signal Framing

Flag:

```text
Autistic behavior is information.
```

Needs:

- careful clinical and disability-rights framing later;
- avoid implying all behavior is safe or consequence-free;
- preserve the existing distinction that behavior should be investigated before it is punished.
