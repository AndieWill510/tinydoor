## 5. Sprint Goal

The Bridge sprint moves the reader from recognition into stakes.

Desired movement:

```text
Doorway recognition
→ why the book exists
→ behavior as signal
→ mortality / lifespan frame without doom
```

The bridge should introduce the public-health stakes without crushing the reader.

---

## 6. Think-Stage Notes Instruction

When reviewing, C may reason privately as needed.

But the requested output is not private chain-of-thought.

Instead, provide concise visible review notes under the heading:

```text
## Think-Stage Notes
```

These notes should be brief, auditable, and useful.

For each target file, include:

```text
- first impression;
- what the Option is trying to do;
- what works;
- reader-safety risks;
- sequencing risks;
- specific repair recommendations;
- promote / promote with repairs / hold recommendation.
```

Do not include hidden chain-of-thought or long internal reasoning.

Give conclusions and short rationale.
