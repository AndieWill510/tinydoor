## 2. Canonical / Emerging Terms

### Stayable

Status: canonical emerging vocabulary.

Working meaning:

```text
a life, room, relationship, practice, schedule, or environment that a nervous system can remain inside without being steadily destroyed by it
```

Editorial note:

Do not casually replace `stayable` with `livable` or `sustainable`.

Those words carry different freight.

`Stayable` keeps the reader close to the book's core question:

> What would help this life become more stayable?
