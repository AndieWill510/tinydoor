## 3. Compression Rule Applied

The pass applied the following rule:

```text
Maslow 1 reader.
Three examples max.
No drone.
No wellness fog.
One reader.
One body.
One door.
```

Operational edits included:

- cutting repeated setup;
- cutting long inventories;
- reducing example lists to three or fewer;
- preserving safety caveats where necessary;
- preserving core memoir lines and authorial heat;
- preserving one small Move per Option;
- preserving mercy without halo excess.

## 8. Process Lesson

Future Options should be drafted from the compressed standard, not expanded and later rescued.

Standing rules:

```text
No more than three examples to make a point.
No tedium masquerading as thoroughness.
No poetic force carrying unsupported claims.
One survivable idea.
One concrete move.
One tiny door.
```
