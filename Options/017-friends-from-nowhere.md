# Option 1.17 — Friends From Nowhere

Author: Kevin "Andie" Williams  
Status: Community section memoir draft  
Project: You Have Another Option  
Source: Andie live dictation  
Safety: memoir / teenage car crash reference / friendship / survival / disclosure review required

---

## Doorway

Friends started arriving from nowhere.

That is not exactly true.

Nobody arrives from nowhere.

There is always a history, a road, a sibling, a room, a terrible car, a thing you survived and almost did not understand as survival at the time.

But that is what it felt like.

Friends from nowhere.

A sister I barely knew.

The sister of my longest friend.

The friend who now lives in Paris.

## Pressure

There was once an ill-advised road trip in a 1987 Acura.

I had negotiated the best price on it, in a car dealership, as a sixteen-year-old.

For fun.

Then I destroyed it.

I did not kill my best friend.

That sentence sits strangely on the page.

It should.

Some friendships carry the weight of what did not happen.

Some futures exist because disaster missed by just enough.

## Reframe

Decades later, the world curved back through a sister he had, who I barely knew.

Community does this sometimes.

It returns through side doors.

Not through the people you expected.

Not through the map you drew.

Through a social thread you did not know was still connected.

A life I thought had been severed still had filaments.

And one of them found me.

## Move

Think of one person who returned through a side door.

One thread.

One filament.

Let it count even if it arrived late.

## Mercy

Not every friendship arrives on time.

Some arrive after you have stopped expecting the old world to send anything back but grief.

And then, somehow, there they are.

Friends from nowhere.

Which is to say: friends from somewhere you did not know still loved you.
