# 100 — Benediction and Closing Thoughts

Author: Kevin "Andie" Williams  
Status: Closing mercy / final benediction  
Project: Tiny Door / You Have Another Option  
Source: Andie direct closing draft  
Evidence: lived experience / source-witness frame  
Safety: closing blessing; no medical, legal, or crisis protocol

---

I started this journey, this little book, three years ago.

I needed it.

I needed a mercy.

Another option.

A pillow fort on the other side of a tiny door.

A haven of community and sanctuary.

So I built one.

And I hope it helps you build your next one.

Never forget:

Whoever has hurt you,

whoever hurts you still —

**they don’t get to win.**
