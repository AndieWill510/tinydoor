## Triage Tier Definitions

|Tier  |Name                              |Description                                                                                                                     |Next action                                      |
|------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
|**T1**|HIGH-STAKES CLAIM                 |Supports a contested clinical, harm, historical, or life-safety claim. Wrong language here hurts people or destroys credibility.|Full evidence map required before use            |
|**T2**|VERIFY CONTENT                    |Source appears relevant but content has not been confirmed. May be right-tier but could be wrong-slot like ref 147.             |Read and confirm before citing                   |
|**T3**|BACKGROUND SCIENCE                |Peer-reviewed science used for conceptual grounding, not a contested claim. Needs accuracy check, not full evidence map.        |Spot-check content matches claim                 |
|**T4**|PRACTICAL SAFETY INFO             |Factual safety information from credible institutional sources. Standard is accuracy and currency, not contested claims.        |Verify still current; check source authority     |
|**T5**|CULTURAL / ILLUSTRATIVE           |Used for framing, analogy, metaphor, or color. Not carrying a factual claim.                                                    |No scrutiny required beyond “is this real”       |
|**T6**|ADVOCACY / LIVED EXPERIENCE       |Legitimate perspective, not peer-reviewed. Must be labeled as such in book.                                                     |Confirm source exists; label correctly in prose  |
|**T7**|JOURNALISM                        |Published reporting, not clinical evidence. Useful for systemic critique, not proof of mechanism.                               |Label as journalism; do not carry clinical claims|
|**T8**|WRONG SLOT / DUPLICATE / UNCLEAR**|Does not match any identifiable claim, is a duplicate, or content is unverifiable from URL alone.                               |Fix, remove, or research before retaining        |
